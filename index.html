Academics Divisions

University Of Abuja enrolls more than 55,364 full- and part-time students throughout eight academic divisions.

Undergraduate

Admission requirements for undergraduate courses at the 100 level are credit passes in five (5) subjects,
including English Language and Mathematics which must be obtained by a candidate at no more than two (2) sittings.
Three (3) of the credit passes must be in subjects relevant to the degree course applied for.
